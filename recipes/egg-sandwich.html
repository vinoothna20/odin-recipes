Egg Sandwich

A delicious microwave egg and cheese sandwich on toast.

It contains 318 calories, 16g fat, 27g carbs and 17g protien

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1 pinch salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 slices white bread
  • 1 slice American cheese

Steps

  1. Crack egg into a microwave-safe bowl; whisk in milk. Season with salt and black pepper. Microwave at 100% power until cooked through, 1 to 2 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, toast bread. Place cooked egg on 1 piece toast; top with cheese slice and other piece toast. Microwave until cheese is melted, about 15 seconds.

That's it, now serve the egg sandwich on your plate.
